Standard Policies

Shoots will be conducted at the client's location. No travel charges apply if the shoot is at the client's premises.

If the client requires outdoor shoots at other locations, travel allowances and accommodation (if required) must be provided.

For PHASES 1, 2 and 3, all outdoor shoots beyond the client's premises will incur travel charges borne by the client.

For PHASES 4 and 5, shoots within a 10 km radius of the client's location are free. Beyond 10 km, travel allowances must be paid by the client.

Travel charges will be calculated based on distance, transport and accommodation (if required), and agreed upon before scheduling.

Freelancing Special Benefit

No travel charges apply for reaching the client's premises within the city (20 km) and returning after the shoot. If travel exceeds this limit, accommodation (if required) will be provided by the client.
